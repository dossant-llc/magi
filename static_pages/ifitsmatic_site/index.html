If it's magic, it belongs to us.

A manifesto for owning our memories, sharing with consent, and building intelligence in the open.

✨ Read the Manifesto 🔗 View the Code

✨ If it's magic, it belongs to us.

Our memories are not commodities. They are the threads of our lives — fragile, timeless, and deeply human.

⏳ If it's magic, it should last.

Knowledge should accumulate with us, not vanish into servers we don't control. What we remember, we keep. What we share, we share by choice.

🔓 If it's magic, it should be free.

Not free as in cost, but free as in freedom — source-available, transparent, and open to exploration.

🛡️ If it's magic, it should respect us.

Privacy is not a feature. It is the foundation. Consent is not optional. It is the rule.

🌟 If it's magic, it should inspire.

This project is not about selling. It is about building in the open, showing what is possible, and reminding ourselves that intelligence can serve humanity without taking from it.

Our Principles

🔒

Ownership

Your memories are yours. They belong to you, not to platforms or corporations.

📜

Consent

Share only with permission. Every interaction respects your choice.

🔎

Transparency

Open code, open trust. See how it works, contribute to its growth.

⏳

Longevity

Knowledge should grow with you, accumulating and lasting through time.

🌟

Inspiration

Build to elevate humanity, showing what's possible when we work together.

If it's magic, it lasts when it's shared.
